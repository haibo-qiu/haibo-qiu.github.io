Haibo Qiu 邱海波

     

I am currently working at Meituan as a Researcher. Before that, I received my PhD degree from the School of Computer Science, University of Sydney (USYD), advised by Prof. Dacheng Tao and co-supervised by Prof. Baosheng Yu. Prior to USYD, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in the Department of Electronic Engineering and Information Science from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

My research interests lie in multi-modality learning, with a particular focus on the mllm post-training, unified multimodal understanding and generation, and multimodal reasoning model.
