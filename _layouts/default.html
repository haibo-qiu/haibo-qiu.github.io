Haibo Qiu (邱海波)

I am a 3nd year PhD student at the School of Computer Science in the Univeristy of Sydney, co-supervised by Dr. Baosheng Yu and Prof. Dacheng Tao. I got my Bachelor's degree in the Department of Electronic Engineering and Information Science from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2018.

My research interests lie on computer vision using deep learning, including 3D human pose estimation, face recognition and 3D semantic segmentation.
